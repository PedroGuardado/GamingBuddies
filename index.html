Welcome to Gaming Buddies

This club welcomes all gamers, regardless of the preferred platform (PlayStation, Nintendo, Xbox or PC). The purpose of this club is to bring gamers together once a week to hang out, play arcade classics and discuss games. Come and make a new "Gaming Buddy".

Event Day, Time and Location

This is a weekly event that takes place at the very popular Happycade. Gamers can enjoy a huge selection of classic arcade games, food and beverages.
